About Craft Market

Craft Market is a student–driven initiative that encourages creativity, entrepreneurship, and skill development. Our platform allows students to showcase their handmade products, connect with buyers, and earn through their talent. Every craft listed here is created with passion, imagination, and dedication.

Whether you are a buyer looking for unique handmade items or a student artist wanting to sell your creations, Craft Market is your perfect destination.
